Rice Pudding

Ingredients:

  • orange or clementine
  • 5 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1L of milk
  • 200 g of round white rice
  • 1 sachet of vanilla sugar or vanilla aroma

Instructions:

  1. Boil the milk with the sugar, the vanilla sugar and the zest of your choice.
  2. When the milk boils, throw in the rice and lower the heat so that it boils very slowly; the rice should cook very slowly.
  3. When the rice comes close to the milk level, turn off the heat and let cool; the rice will finish soaking up the milk as it cools.
  4. This dessert can be served warm or cold, depending on your taste.

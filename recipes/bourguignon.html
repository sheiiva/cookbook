Seitan Bourguignon

Ingredients:

  • 2 squares of dark chocolate
  • 500 ml of vegetable stock
  • 1 tablespoon of flour
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 70 ml of Bourgogne red wine
  • 500 g of seitan
  • 300 g of mushrooms
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 20 g of margarine
  • 1 bouquet garni
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. The day before or the morning for dinner:
  2. Rinse the vegetables and finely chop the garlic, onion, and mushrooms. Cut the carrots into 1 cm pieces.
  3. In a pot over medium heat, melt the margarine. Add the garlic, onion, carrots, and mushrooms. Saut√© for 5 minutes.
  4. Cut the seitan into thick cubes, similar to stew meat.
  5. Add the seitan to the pot and let it sear for 2 minutes.
  6. Deglaze with all the red wine, scraping the bits off the bottom with a spatula.
  7. Add the bouquet garni and remove from heat.
  8. Cover and let marinate overnight.
  9. The next day, pour the vegetable stock into the pot. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. Cook again, simmering for 1 hour over medium-low heat. Before serving, add the squares of dark chocolate and stir until melted to bind the sauce.
  11. It's ready! Serve with rice or egg-free pasta.

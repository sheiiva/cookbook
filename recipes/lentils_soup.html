Lentils Soup

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrots
  • 1 leek white
  • 1 shallot
  • 150g green lentils
  • 2 bouillon cubes
  • 1L of water

Instructions:

  1. Peel the white leek, carrot, onion and shallot, and cut into mirepoix (small dice).
  2. In a casserole, put three tablespoons of olive oil and sweat the vegetables which will for 5 to 10 minutes over low heat.
  3. Add the lentils, mixing them well with the vegetables so that they become translucent and absorb the flavors of the vegetables.
  4. Add 1 liter of water, two stock cubes, and simmer for 3/4 of an hour.
  5. Mix the preparation (no food mill, the mixer leaves small pieces for a nicer consistency).
  6. Serve hot with croutons, a little grated cheese, or boiled eggs.
